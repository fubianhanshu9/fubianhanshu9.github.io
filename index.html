Biography

Xu Du is a postdoctoral researcher under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojun Yuan at the National Key Laboratory of Science and Technology on Communications of University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC). He received his B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from Zhengzhou University (ZZU) in 2017, and Ph.D under the supervision of Prof. Boris Houska in Engineering Communication and Information Systems from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and Shanghaitech University in 2022. His research interests include distributed optimization, game theory, optimal experiement design, optimal control, robust optimization, wireless communication and power grid.
