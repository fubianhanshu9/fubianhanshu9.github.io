Biography

Xu Du is an assistant researcher of the Institute of Mathematics HNAS, Henan Academy of Science. He received his B.E. in Computer Science and Technology from Zhengzhou University (ZZU) in 2017, and Ph.D under the supervision of Prof. Boris Houska in Engineering Communication and Information Systems from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) and ShanghaiTech University in 2022. He was a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Xiaojun Yuan at the National Key Laboratory of Science and Technology on Communications of University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) from 2023.1 to 2023.6. His research interests include distributed optimization, game theory, optimal experiement design, optimal control, robust optimization, wireless communication and power grid.
