Biography

Shuting Wu is a master student of North China University of Water Resources and Electric Power and Henan Acamedy of Science, under the supervision of Xu Du and Mohammad Javad Habibi. She received the B.S. degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Anyang Normal University, China, and is currently pursuing the M.S. degree in Mathematics at North China University of Water Resources and Electric Power. Her research interests include distributed learning algorithms and model predictive control.
